What changed vs Rank 1 — and why

  1. Echo removed. The aggregate number (2,418) and the rising curve now appear once, in the hero. citegeist's figures (1,043 / 38) live only in its story panel — they're gone from the project cards, so nothing is stated three times. Stars dropped as a co-anchor; "247 stars" survives only in the offscreen data table.
  2. Reordered for a non-technical reader. A plain-language sentence leads ("software people actually use — and it keeps growing"), then one big number with a human caption ("times people installed something I shipped"), then a "plain terms" strip that translates the jargon. A recruiter gets it before meeting the words star or fork.
  3. Award-grade composition. One confident hero, large Fraunces statement, a single instrument-grade numeral, generous air, atmospheric swell gradient. Reads as craft, not a dashboard.
  4. Whole canvas used. Full-bleed page (1240px content max), clamp-based editorial type up to 184px, section rhythm on the 56–160px steps, projects as crafted cards instead of a dense ledger.
